Refund Policy

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds under the following circumstances:

  • Technical failure preventing analysis delivery
  • Service unavailability during the analysis process
  • Double charging or incorrect amount charged
  • Request made within 24 hours of purchase, before analysis is delivered

2. Non-Refundable Circumstances

Refunds will not be provided in these cases:

  • After the analysis has been delivered successfully
  • If you've already used the re-upload attempts
  • Dissatisfaction with the analysis recommendation
  • Requests made after 24 hours of purchase

3. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at support@aivalidator.com
  2. Provide your payment ID and reason for refund
  3. Include any relevant screenshots or error messages

We will process eligible refund requests within 5-7 business days.

4. Refund Method

Refunds will be processed through the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Card payments: 5-7 business days
  • UPI payments: 1-3 business days
  • Net Banking: 3-5 business days

5. Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds:

  • When partial service has been delivered
  • When compensating for service interruption
  • For technical issues affecting service quality

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@aivalidator.com
  • Support Hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours on business days

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page.
