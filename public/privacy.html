Privacy Policy

At AI Agent vs Script Validator, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our service:

We use your information to:

Your project descriptions are processed by:

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

6. Data Security

We implement strong security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
